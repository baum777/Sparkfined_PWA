### Breakpoints
- **Mobile (< 768px)**: Single column layout
  - KPI cards: 1 per row
  - Buttons: flex-wrap, full-width on small screens
  
- **Tablet (768px - 1024px)**: 2-column KPI grid
  - Action buttons: 2-3 per row

- **Desktop (> 1024px)**: 3-column KPI grid
  - Max-width: 1152px (max-w-6xl)
