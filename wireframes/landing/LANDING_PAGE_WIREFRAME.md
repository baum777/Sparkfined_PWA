## Interaction Patterns

### Navigation
- **Fixed Top Nav:** Always visible, transparent with blur on scroll
- **CTA Buttons:** Emerald glow effect, scale on hover
- **Smooth Scroll:** Anchor links scroll smoothly to sections

### Animations
- **Hero Entrance:** Staggered fade-in (headline â†’ subtext â†’ CTAs)
- **Ticker:** Infinite horizontal scroll (30s loop)
- **Feature Cards:** Lift and glow on hover (desktop only)
- **Stats Counter:** Count up from 0 on scroll into view
- **Testimonials:** Auto-rotate every 5s, pause on hover

### Touch Interactions (Mobile)
- **Tap Targets:** 44px minimum (WCAG AA compliant)
- **Swipe Testimonials:** Horizontal swipe between cards
- **Pull-to-Refresh:** Disabled (not needed for static page)

### Micro-interactions
- **CTA Button:** Pulse glow (2s loop)
- **Feature Cards:** Icon animates on hover
- **Stats:** Shimmer effect on hover
- **Testimonials:** Subtle scale on active card
