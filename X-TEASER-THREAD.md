**Voice & Tone:**
- Professional but approachable
- Technical but not jargon-heavy

- Data-driven (specific numbers, benchmarks)

- Speed (measurable: seconds, MB)
- Simplicity (no sign-up, no install friction)
- Transparency (building in public, open roadmap)
- Community (feedback-driven, open to collaboration)
